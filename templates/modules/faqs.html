Basic Questions

Yes, you can upload your resume to the portal. After logging into your account, navigate to the "My Profile" section and click on the "Upload Resume" button. Follow the prompts to select and upload your resume file. This will make it easier for employers to find and review your qualifications when you apply for jobs on the portal.

To sign up for an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the required information, including your email address, password, and basic personal details. Once you submit the form, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

After you submit your application, it undergoes a review process by the hiring team or recruiter. This review process may vary depending on the employer's recruitment procedures. Typically, your application is screened to ensure it meets the job requirements and qualifications. If your application is selected for further consideration, you may be contacted for an interview or additional assessments.

Ensure that your resume is saved in a compatible file format, such as PDF, DOC, or DOCX. If your resume is in a different format, try converting it to one of these formats and then attempt to upload it again.

Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who require access for legitimate business purposes. We enforce strict access controls and authentication mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access to your data.

Search for Jobs: Start by browsing the available job listings on our portal. You can use filters to narrow down your search based on criteria such as location, industry, or job type. Review Job Details: Click on a job listing to view more details about the position, including the job description, requirements, and application instructions. Make sure the job aligns with your skills and interests before proceeding. Submit Your Application: If you're interested in applying for the job, follow the application instructions provided in the job listing. This may involve filling out an online application form, uploading your resume and cover letter, or providing additional documents or information requested by the employer. Review and Confirm: Before submitting your application, review all the information you've provided to ensure it's accurate and complete. Double-check for any spelling or formatting errors in your resume and cover letter. Submit Application: Once you're satisfied with your application, click the "Submit" or "Apply" button to send it to the employer. Your application will be forwarded to the hiring team for review.
